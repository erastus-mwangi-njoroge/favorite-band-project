Krystaal Gospel Music Band

Krystaal is a gospel music band composed of three brothers; Fabian, Michael and Aliston who share the surname Lwamba. Currently, they live in Toronto, Canada. They tour the world spreading the gospel of forgiveness and hope for the broken-hearted. These artists consider music as a therapy to life hardships, having spent their past as refugees. They freed from Congo while in university in Congo in 1990.

Michael and Aliston were hosted in one refugee camp differnt from Fabian. As they excercised their talent, they got sponshorship abroad and they shared the experience in a video to the rest of the refugees in their former refugee camp. Coincidentally, Fabian had been relocated to this camp and after seeing this video, he got a basis for reuniting with his brothers. Two years later, Fabian was sponsored by the same family that had hosted his brothers. After reuniting, they were able to launch the Krystaal music band. More information on their journey is recorded in globalnews website.

Bibliography of the artists

Michael Lwamba

Michael is the oldest of the three brothers. He began interest in music when he was as twelve years when he made his own guitar after his father had failed to buy him one. Fabian and Aliston show the passion and joined in making the vocal styles that later gave birth to Krystall band. Michael saved his wife,sisters and brother Aliston during Mobutu Seseko's arnachist rule.
